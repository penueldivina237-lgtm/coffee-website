About Bean & Brew

Bean & Brew is a specialty coffee shop and micro-roastery. We source single-origin beans, roast them in small batches, and craft drinks that highlight flavor and balance.

Our space doubles as a community hub where you can enjoy expertly made espresso, seasonal filter brews, and house-baked pastries. Stop by for a tasting, a bag of fresh beans, or a cozy seat to get some work done.
